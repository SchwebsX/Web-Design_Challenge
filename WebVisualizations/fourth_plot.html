This is City Latitude vs Wind Speed (01/05/17)

This work is such an abrupt departure from the previous works, it is hard to imagine

that is from the same hand. There was speculation that the artist commissioned a local

beekeeper to do the work, but that has never been proven. On it's face it seems simiplistic,

almost childlike. But this simplicity belies it's profound nature as a deeply sentimental and

emotional relfection of love.
