This is City Latitude vs Humidity (01/15/17)

Where the first work was whimsical then self-concious, this second work stand alone and doesn't need the viewer.

It's starkness, small dots cascading down, leave the viewer almost embarrased to view. As if you were watching

something you were not supposed to be watching. The poet Mura-Knosis likened to stumbling upon an old woman on a

bridge who might be feeding fish, or might be about to jump. Mura-Knosis was always such a cut up though.
