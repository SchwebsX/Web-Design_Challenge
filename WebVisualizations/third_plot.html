This is City Latitude vs Cloudiness (01/05/17)

The flowing, natural forms of work 1 and 2 are nothing like the rigid, structural form of work 3

This work, completed during the artist's self imposed house arrest protesting why the hot water was

always on the right, shows that even in harsh confinement the human spirit can flourish. The 8th dot

the left stands as a tribute, a beacon of hope to people oppressed by plumbing conformity worldwide
